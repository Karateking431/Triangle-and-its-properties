The Many Types Of Triangles

Exploring shapes that build our world

Equilateral Triangle

All three sides are equal. All three angles are also equal (each is 60°). It looks perfectly balanced.

Isosceles Triangle

Two sides are equal. The angles opposite those sides are equal. Very common in roofs and signs.

Scalene Triangle

All sides are different. All angles are different too. No symmetry at all.

Acute-angled Triangle

All three angles are less than 90°. Every corner is sharp.

Right-angled Triangle

One angle is exactly 90°. Very important in maths and real life. Used in buildings, ramps, ladders.

Obtuse-angled Triangle

One angle is more than 90°. Looks slightly stretched.
